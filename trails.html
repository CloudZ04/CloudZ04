Bike Trails

Bike Trail Information

Wilderness Forest is a mountain biking Mecca deep in the heart of the stunning Borders. The biking experience here includes Green, Blue, Red or Black graded trails, as well as a magnificent multi-graded free-ride area.

In the unlikely event that Wilderness fails to meet your thirst for biking exhilaration, you are only a short hop from Innerleithen's famous Red Bull down-hill and cross-country trails.
