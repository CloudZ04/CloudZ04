Bike Hire Information

Welcome to Bike King Borders, where our bike hire services are designed to elevate your cycling experience. Whether you're an avid mountain biker, a road cycling enthusiast, or introducing your junior rider to the joys of biking, we have a diverse range of high-quality bikes to meet your needs.

ID is required to hire our bikes. A passport or drivers licence along with a credit card deposit.

Over 18’s must sign for and supervise under 16’s.

16 and 17-year-olds can ride unsupervised but cannot supervise under 16’s.

All bikes are to be returned/collected by 5pm or a second day will be charged.
